The 5 Design Methods

Likes and Wishes

Likes and Wishes helps you express what you like and wish about the topic you have chosen and convert them into opportunities.

Opportunities

Synthesize your likes and wishes to identify potential opportunities around the topics to work on.

Cheatstorming

No idea is original. Cheatstorming is an ideation method that helps generate new ideas inspired by old ideas.

Voting

Vote for your favorite ideas that address your opportunity statement.

Concept Development

Convert your ideas into detailed concepts! Mash your ideas with concepts from your peers and create new ones.
